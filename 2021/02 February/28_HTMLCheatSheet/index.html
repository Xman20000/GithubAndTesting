Cry me crocodile tear river:
Hey there! I've been dead for a while, But I want to get better. I admit to the endless void of no-one that I am not ok and I know that... And my main issue is that I don't do anything about that even if I want to...
So since it's been so long, I am restarting again from the beginning, hoping to do things right... or at least to an acceptable degree.
Hey, this is me after making this look a little better to the eyes. It's my own fault that I haven't been very active as of late, many wasted hours, days, weeks, months, years... But the only way foward is to create something... anything...
Well I am trying to put the past behind me, for I have been stuck in an endless cycle for 4 years now... almost 5 now... It's been too long. Take care you beautiful people, may you have a great morning, evening, or well, have a great day. May you follow your dreams and bring forth a better future. I might be lost but it's not too late for you. Best of luck in your travels, Adios.
